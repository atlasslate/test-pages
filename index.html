❓ FAQ

The device won't connect?

Ensure your USB-C cable handles data (test it with a smartphone). Many cables are power-only. If it still fails, hold the BOOT button on the side while plugging it in.

Screen is frozen?

E-Ink requires a full refresh cycle to clear ghosting. Quick-press the RST button once to trigger a screen refresh.
