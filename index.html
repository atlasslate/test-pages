Meshtastic Network

Your Sat Slate hardware is fully compatible with the open-source Meshtastic communication network. You can overwrite your firmware to use it for off-grid messaging.

⚠️ CRITICAL WARNING

You MUST attach the included LoRa antenna BEFORE flashing Meshtastic.

Meshtastic activates the radio transmitter immediately upon booting. Transmitting without an antenna attached will cause the radio signals to bounce back into the board, permanently destroying the LoRa chip.
